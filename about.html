Our CHOICE Values

C ommunication – we will actively listen to your needs and proactively plan supports with you, for you.

H onesty & Integrity – you can trust us to support you proactively with reliability.

O pportunity & Passion – we will work with you collaboratively to provide supports of your choice so you can achieve your desired outcomes.

I nnovation & Quality – we believe in creating new and exciting opportunities whilst continually improving the services we provide, by listening to you.

C ourage – we proudly embrace the culture of acceptance and inclusiveness for all.

E mpowerment – we will support you in expressing your needs and to make informed decisions about your supports

VISION

To empower individuals living with a disability to live their best life the way they choose within an inclusive society.

MISSION

To offer a high quality, reliable and person-centred support to individuals living with a disability.
