Services Provided
Mentoring/ Individualised Support

Supports to enable you to engage in community, social and recreational activities of your choice which is provided on an individual basis.

Contact US
Short Term Accommodation (STA)/ Respite

We provide short term accommodation tailored to your needs and preferences, providing you with opportunities to relax, to participate in a variety of activities and make new friends in a supportive environment

Contact US
Supported Independent Living (SIL) & Voluntary Out of Home Care (VOHC)

We have highly skilled and experienced staff to support you 24/7 in your own home, assisting with all elements of your daily life inclusive of personal care, daily living skills, community activities and transport.

Contact US
Positive Behaviour Support

Positive Behaviour Support focuses on evidence-based strategies and person centred supports that address the needs of the person with a disability and the underlying causes of behaviours of concern, while safeguarding the dignity and quality of life of people with a disability who require specialist behaviour support.

Contact US
Psychosocial Recovery Support

Our psychosocial recovery coaches will work with you to assist you in identifying your personal goals and to make plans to reach these whilst addressing the barriers. We will support you in making valuable connections within your community to assist with empowering you to manage your day to day life.

Contact US
Social & Skill Development Groups

Variety of social groups for children, teenagers & adults with a focus on developing life & social skills whilst having fun and exploring new interests in a supportive environment.

Contact US
Day Options

Our Day Options program provides a range of recreational and social activities designed to increase independence, support the development of social and communication skills, build confidence and self-esteem all while having fun.

Contact US
Support Coordination

Our experienced Support Coordinators can work with you to provide assistance in navigating the NDIS and the many services available. We will source various options so that you can choose the best possible supports to meet your personal needs

Contact US
School Holiday Activities

Programs are designed to give children and teens a social outlet whilst they are on school holidays. The programs are a mixture of community based activities, recreational activities at our centre and opportunities to develop and improve daily living skills.

Contact US
In-home Support

Support provided to you in your own home to assist you in areas such as shopping, food preparation, cooking, cleaning or attending appointments.
